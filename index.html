THE SHIFFON STORY


Shiffon is a fine jewelry brand founded by Harvard undergrads Jennifer Wang and Shilpa Yarlagadda. Although Jennifer and Shilpa grew up in the same neighborhood, they came from vastly different cultures: Chinese and Indian.  What they share is a passion for fine jewelry. As children, they dug through their mothers’ jewelry boxes to uncover one-of-a-kind pieces never to be found again. Until now. After crossing paths at Harvard, Jennifer and Shilpa founded Shiffon with the mission to create timeless modern jewelry infused with special elements from their cultures. ​

Embued with humanitarian passion, Shiffon shares half its profit with the Startup Girl Foundation, the nonprofit arm of Shiffon. The Startup Girl Foundation provides young female entrepreneurs with seed grants and a strong network of mentors — the foundation to startup success. Shiffon is the story of two young female entrepreneurs taking a risk to turn their passion into a reality. Through the Startup Girl Foundation, Jennifer and Shilpa want to support and empower others to do the same.
